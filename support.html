Support

Help, bugs, and beta feedback.

Cuehold is an early iPhone beta focused on capture, triage, and deciding what to do next. External TestFlight access is not public yet.

Current support contact: email monop2511@gmail.com or use TestFlight feedback from the Cuehold beta app for bugs, privacy questions, and beta feedback.

Email support What to include Beta status

For beta testers

Use TestFlight feedback when available. Include screenshots or screen recordings if they help explain the issue.

What to include

Include your iPhone model, iOS version, TestFlight build number, what you captured, what you expected, and what happened instead.

Useful categories

  • Capture issue
  • Screenshot/share issue
  • Voice/transcription issue
  • Today Signal / Up Next issue
  • Reminder export issue
  • Calendar export issue
  • Privacy/permission concern
  • Crash/performance issue

Out of scope

Medical support, emergency help, public App Store billing, and fully automated scheduling are not part of the current beta.
