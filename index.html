How Cuehold is different

Before Calendar. Before Reminders.

Calendar tells you where your time is going. Reminders tells you what you committed to. Cuehold helps decide what something is before it becomes either.

Cuehold asks: what is this, does it matter, and what should I do next? It should not duplicate everything across Apple Calendar and Reminders.

  • Use Calendar when something has a real time.
  • Use Reminders when something is a commitment.
  • Use Cuehold when something is still messy, ambiguous, half-formed, or easy to lose.
  • Cuehold should reduce duplicates, not create another place to maintain everything forever.
