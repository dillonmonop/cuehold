Privacy

Privacy, simple not simplistic.

Cuehold is a productivity capture and triage app. It helps you capture messy inputs, clean them up, and decide what should happen next.

Static website

This GitHub Pages site does not add analytics, ads, or payment tools. Beta access uses Apple TestFlight.

Productivity, not medical care

Cuehold is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, therapy, clinical advice, or ADHD treatment.

Content you choose

Cuehold is designed around content you intentionally type, paste, speak, import, capture, or share into the app.

Content that may be processed

This may include typed captures, pasted text, voice transcript text, OCR text from screenshots, shared content text, and screenshot/image input.

OpenAI processing

If AI-assisted cleanup or routing is enabled or used, selected content may be processed by OpenAI. This may include typed text, pasted text, voice transcript text, OCR text from screenshots, shared content text, and screenshot/image input. Cuehold uses this processing to generate suggested saved items and next actions.

Why content is processed

Cuehold uses selected content to clean up messy captures, suggest next actions, generate suggested saved items, and route captures toward Reminder or Calendar review where appropriate.

Apple Calendar and Reminders

Calendar access may be used for context and user-confirmed exports. Reminders access may be used for user-confirmed exports. Cuehold does not silently create, edit, complete, delete, or move Apple Calendar events or Apple Reminders.

User control

You choose what you capture or share into Cuehold. Exports require confirmation. You can delete local Cuehold items where supported.

Beta feedback and support

Beta installation and feedback may be handled through TestFlight. Beta reports may include device details, screenshots, reproduction notes, and anything you choose to send. Contact support for privacy questions.
