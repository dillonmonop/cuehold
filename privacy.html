Draft privacy notice

Privacy

This is a draft privacy page for beta preparation. Final privacy wording will be reviewed before External TestFlight.

What the app may handle

Typed captures, pasted text, voice-derived text, screenshots or shared content, local app data, and user-approved Apple Calendar or Reminder exports.

User control

The app is designed so Calendar and Reminder exports require review and confirmation before anything is created.

AI processing

If AI cleanup is used, the app will explain what content may be processed and ask for consent first.
