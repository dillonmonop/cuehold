Privacy

Privacy, simple not simplistic.

Cuehold is still in beta preparation. This page explains the current privacy posture without pretending the product is publicly launched.

Static website

This GitHub Pages site is static. It does not add analytics, tracking scripts, ads, or payment tools.

Captures you choose

The app is designed around content you intentionally capture, paste, speak, import, or share into Cuehold.

Screenshots and OCR

Screenshot text extraction is used to help you review and triage images you choose to provide.

Voice notes

Voice capture and transcription are used only when you choose to record or process spoken input.

Apple Reminders and Calendar

Cuehold uses Apple services only for user-approved exports, context, or actions you choose.

AI-assisted cleanup

If optional AI processing is used, Cuehold should make that clear before selected content is processed.

Beta feedback

Beta reports may include device details, screenshots, reproduction notes, and anything you choose to send.

Not medical advice

Cuehold is a workflow tool. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or therapy.
